Marin High School

it was established in 1954

The school is run under Chevalier T Thomas Educational Trust (Regd.), a public Charitable Trust, started by the founderChev. T Thomas. The Trust is a leading Educational agency which already has in its fold four recognized leading institutions in Chennai – Matric Boys’ Hr. Sec. School and Matric Girls’ Hr. Sec. School in PERAMBUR, Chennai, Matric Hr. Sec. School (Co-educational) at Redhills and a women’s college in Perambur. The three schools are affiliated to the Matriculation and Hr. Sec. Boards of Govt. of Tamilnadu and the college is affiliated to the University of Madras. Marian Sr. Sec. School is the only school in Kerala under the Management which is affiliated to the Central Board of Sec. Education, New Delhi. The object of the Trust is to establish and manage Educational institutions of high standard and to serve the society and nation by helping the needy and deserving students irrespective of Caste, Community, Creed or Religion.>

Marin High School Libary

it was established in 1978

The library is well equipped with more than 5000 volumes of books in each school for reference and study by the students. Periodicals and Magazines are also procured to cater to the needs of all levels of students and staff members.

Marin High School Hostel

it was established in 1964

Hostel facilities limited to restricted number of students. Hostel accommodation is available only for girls .Those who would like to avail hostel facilities may contact the Principal. Application form can be collected from the school office.
